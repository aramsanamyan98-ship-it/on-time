# 01 — Vision

## What On-Time Is

On-Time is a booking and growth platform for independent barbers and beauty
specialists in Armenia. It replaces phone calls, Instagram DMs, and memory
with a simple online calendar — and gives specialists a public profile where
new clients can discover them.

On-Time is **not** primarily a scheduling tool. Scheduling is the entry
feature. The actual product is discovery + growth for specialists who don't
have one yet.

## Who It's For (v1)

- Independent barbers renting a chair in a shared shop
- Small barbershops (1–10 barbers)
- Nail specialists and cosmetologists working from home or a small studio
- Specifically: people **early in building their client base**, not
  established shops with a full book already

## Who It's Not For (v1)

- Large, already-famous salons with a full existing clientele (we may list
  them for free later, but they are not the paying wedge)
- Businesses needing multi-location management, staff payroll, or inventory
- Anyone requiring online payments or deposits in v1

## The Core Insight

Existing local tools (SetTime, Altegio) solve scheduling for specialists who
*already have clients*. Nobody in the Armenian market is helping a
just-started specialist **get discovered**. That's the wedge.

## Positioning Statement

> For barbers and beauty specialists starting to build their name in
> Armenia, who have no way to be found beyond word of mouth and can't
> afford software before they have steady income, On-Time is a booking
> platform that helps them get discovered and get paid without giving up
> hours to phone calls and DMs. Unlike SetTime and Altegio, which serve
> specialists who already have a client base, On-Time grows with you from
> your first client to your fiftieth — free while you're building,
> affordable once you've made it.

## Brand Promise

We promise that barbers and beauty professionals will not waste time
talking to clients. We also promise not to charge them before they're
actually earning from the platform.

## Mission

To provide barbers with a simple digital tool that organizes appointments,
reduces interruptions, and helps them run their business more efficiently.

## Long-Term Vision

To become the leading discovery and booking platform for beauty
professionals in Armenia, then expand to:
- Beauty salons
- Tattoo artists
- Nail technicians
- Dentists and other appointment-based service providers

## v1 Scope Boundaries (confirmed decisions)

These are locked for v1 — do not build beyond them without updating this
doc first:

| Question | v1 Decision |
|---|---|
| Customer accounts | Guest-only booking. No customer login in v1. Account creation is a future phase. |
| Payments | In-shop only. No online payment processing in v1. |
| Deposits / anti-no-show | Not in v1. Reminders only. Deposits are a v2 feature. |
| Languages | Armenian, Russian, and English — all three from day one. |
| Domain structure | Marketing site on `ontime.am` (Webflow). App on `app.ontime.am`. Public booking pages on `book.ontime.am/[specialist-slug]`. |

## Core Values

- **Simplicity** — barbers are busy; the product must be extremely easy to use
- **Reliability** — appointments must always work, no crashes or confusion
- **Respect for the craft** — specialists are artists and professionals, not just "users"
- **Efficiency** — help specialists save time and reduce stress
- **Community** — support the specialist community and help them grow
